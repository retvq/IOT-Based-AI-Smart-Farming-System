Pesticides for Tomato

Season Months Pests Pestisides Image
Year-round, but more common in warm and humid conditions. Throughout the growing season. Whiteflies (Bemisia Tabaci) Neem-based products, insecticidal soaps, pyrethroids.
Cool to warm seasons. Late fall to early spring. Aphids (Aphididae) Neonicotinoids, pyrethroids, insecticidal soaps.
Warm season. Late spring to early fall. Tomato Fruit Borer (Helicoverpa Armigera) Pesticide: Bacillus Thuringiensis (Bt), Spinosad.
Warm season. Late spring to early fall. Leafhoppers Pesticide: Neonicotinoids, pyrethroids.
